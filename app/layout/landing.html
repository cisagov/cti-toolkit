Welcome to the STIXTM Editor

The Structured Threat Information eXpression (STIX) Editor is designed to simplify the creation of STIX documents that can be submitted to the National Cybersecurity and Communications Integration Center (NCCIC) for use in the Automated Indicator Sharing (AIS) capability.

This STIX Editor is intended for use on Firefox browsers. Results and experiences may vary with other browsers.

All questions or concerns regarding this STIX Editor, please send them to: ncciccustomerservice@hq.dhs.gov
